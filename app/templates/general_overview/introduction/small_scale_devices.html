Small Scale Devices

Small-scale integration is sufficient to implement basic logic functions such as AND and OR gates. SSI chips typically contain one to ten gate equivalents per chip (2-50 components). A garage door opener is a common example of a device that uses small-scale integration.

SSI devices are used in a variety of applications, including:

  • Automotive electronics
  • Industrial control systems
  • Consumer electronics
  • Medical devices

SSI devices are also used in the following applications:

  • Remote controls
  • Security systems
  • Home automation systems
  • Appliances
