Chapter Review - Introduction

Three stages in the history of Electronics are:

  • Vacuum Tubes
  • Discrete Components
  • Integrated Circuits

Four stages in the development of Integrated Circuits are:

  • Small Scale Integration
  • Medium Scale Integration
  • Large Scale Integration
  • Very Large Scale Integration

Three advantages of integrated circuits over discrete components are:

  • Reduced Size
  • Reduced Power Requirements
  • Increased Reliability

Two basic types of IC devices are:

  • Analog
  • Digital
