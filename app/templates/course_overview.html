Semiconductor Overview

This application provides an overview of the semiconductor fabrication process.

The materials are organized into two levels, each divided into chapters.

  • General Overview covers the basic concepts of semiconductor fabrication.
  • Technical Overview provides more detailed information on each step of the process.

Each chapter is designed to stand alone, allowing chapters to be taken in any order.

It is recommended that students follow the chapters in order.

Each chapter includes:

  • A brief introduction
  • Objectives
  • A summary

There are also quizzes to test your knowledge.

It is recommended that students complete the quizzes after each chapter.

There are also links to additional resources for further study.

It is recommended that students explore these resources to deepen their understanding.
